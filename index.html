Jedi Mind Mocha

This decadent drink starts with a base of rich espresso, which is then combined with velvety steamed milk and a generous swirl of chocolate syrup. Topped with a dollop of whipped cream and a sprinkle of cocoa powder, the Jedi Mind Mocha is a comforting and indulgent treat that will awaken your senses and keep you focused on your quest.

Sith Dark Side Latte

Embrace the dark side with this bold and intense latte. Featuring a double shot of espresso and steamed milk, the Sith Dark Side Latte is infused with a splash of black cherry syrup and finished with a dramatic drizzle of dark chocolate sauce. With its powerful flavor and striking presentation, this drink is sure to satisfy even the most formidable of palates.

Wookiee Caramel Macchiato

Get ready to roar with delight over this delicious twist on a classic favorite. The Wookiee Caramel Macchiato combines smooth espresso with creamy steamed milk and a hint of vanilla syrup, all topped off with a generous drizzle of caramel sauce. Served hot or iced, this drink is as comforting as a hug from your favorite Wookiee friend and is sure to leave you feeling warm and fuzzy inside.
